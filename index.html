I am driven by tackling the most intriguing challenges in product development. With a Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering and over 14 years in technical program management, I bring expertise across diverse domains.

Starting in materials science research, I discovered my passion for innovative tech products during my tenure as a failure analysis engineer. Transitioning into a consulting TPM role for Big Tech clients, I focused on pioneering next-generation products. Notably, I directed global-scale data programs for creating and curating training data sets for algorithm development. Later, at Cruise, I worked to solve complex AI and operational challenges in commercializing autonomous vehicle technology.

My approach to effective teams and program management centers on maximizing impact. I focus on creating valuable work products, streamlining processes to integrate seamlessly with current workflows, and ensuring purposeful meetings. I am proactive about identifying, engaging with, and escalating new opportunities. I believe that clear leadership and decision-making frameworks empower teams to drive innovation and focus on their most significant value adds.
